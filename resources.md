<h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Data Science in Public Affairs</h2>

<p style="white-space:pre-wrap;">We are compiling resources for faculty and students in public policy working with R. These include pedagogical resources for students doing graduate-level work in MPA and MPP programs, PhD seminars that share data programming resources, and data science methods within the field of public policy that use R.&nbsp;</p>
